Detection Information
  • EAR (Eye Aspect Ratio) Measures eye openness. Low values indicate closed eyes.
  • MAR (Mouth Aspect Ratio) Detects yawning when ratio exceeds threshold.
  • Yaw/Pitch Head orientation to detect distraction and head nodding.
  • Alerts Red text indicates drowsiness, yawning, or distraction detected.
  • EAR Threshold: 0.280 (Default)
  • MAR Threshold: 0.800 (Default)
  • Pitch Threshold: 10.0° (Default)
  • Yaw Threshold: 20.0° (Default)
